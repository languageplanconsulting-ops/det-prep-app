โครงสร้างเรียงความ (Essay scaffold)

1

"[Topic] has become a popular alternative to [traditional ___]."

เกริ่นนำ — ให้ context ของหัวข้อก่อนเลย

2

"The benefits of ___ include ___, ___, and ___."

ข้อดี — list ข้อดีหลัก 3 ข้อ

3

"Additionally, ___ also enables [people] to develop ___ skills."

เสริมข้อดี — ขยายความด้วย enable / allow

4

"However, ___ also has some drawbacks. Firstly, ___, which can lead to feelings of isolation or detachment. Secondly, ___."

ข้อเสีย — ยอมรับข้อเสีย 2 ข้อ (ให้ balanced)

5

"Despite these drawbacks, ___ has become an increasingly viable option for ___."

สรุป — concede แต่ conclude ข้าง positive

โครงสร้างเรียงความ (Essay scaffold)

1

"In my opinion, ___."  /  "I strongly believe that ___."

บอกจุดยืนทันที — อย่าลังเล

2

"Firstly, ___. For example, ___."

เหตุผล 1 + ตัวอย่างรองรับ

3

"Secondly, ___."

เหตุผล 2 — เสริมให้แข็งแกร่ง

4

"On the other hand, some people argue that ___. However, ___."

ยอมรับอีกมุม แล้ว counter — ทำให้ essay ดู balanced

5

"In conclusion, I believe that ___ because ___."

สรุปย้ำจุดยืน — echo intro

📚 คำ & วลีที่ใช้บ่อย ▼
in my opinion I strongly believe from my perspective there is no doubt that it is undeniable that play a crucial role the advantages outweigh the disadvantages compelling beneficial
✏️ จุดไวยากรณ์ที่ควรใช้ ▼
"While some argue that …, I believe …"
Comparatives — "better than / more important than"
"not only … but also …"

โครงสร้างเรียงความ (Essay scaffold)

1

"One [place/person/object] that holds special meaning to me is ___."

เกริ่นถึงสิ่งนั้น — ตั้งชื่อมันก่อนเลย

2

"It is ___ / He/She is ___. Whenever I ___, I ___."

บรรยายรายละเอียด + ความสัมพันธ์

3

"What makes it special is ___."

ทำไมพิเศษ — cleft sentence ดูดีมาก

4

"Being there / With them, I feel ___."

ความรู้สึก — ใช้ adjective หลากหลาย

5

"Overall, ___ will always hold a special place in my heart."

สรุป — idiomatic, sounds natural

📚 คำ & วลีที่ใช้บ่อย ▼
holds special meaning sentimental value evokes memories of a sense of (peace / belonging) cherish remarkable serene nostalgic vivid admire

📄 ตัวอย่างคำตอบ — Benefits & drawbacks of social media ▼

B1 sample (~65 words)

Social media has become a popular way to communicate. The benefits include staying in touch with friends and family, sharing information quickly, and meeting new people. However, it also has drawbacks. Some people spend too much time on their phones, which can lead to feelings of isolation. Additionally, there is a lot of false information online. Despite these problems, social media is useful if we use it carefully.

simple vocabulary ✓ clear structure ✓ limited connectors

B2 sample (~100 words)

Social media has become a popular alternative to traditional face-to-face communication. The benefits include instant connection with people across the world, access to a wide range of information, and the ability to share ideas freely. Additionally, social media enables people to develop digital communication skills that are increasingly valued in the workplace. However, it also has some drawbacks. Firstly, excessive use can lead to feelings of isolation and reduced attention spans. Secondly, the prevalence of misinformation poses a serious challenge. Despite these drawbacks, social media remains an increasingly viable option for staying informed and connected.

varied vocabulary ✓ good connectors ✓ balanced argument ✓

C1 sample (~130 words)

Social media has fundamentally transformed the way people interact, becoming a popular alternative to conventional forms of communication. Among its most compelling benefits is the ability to forge and sustain connections across geographical boundaries, enabling individuals to access diverse perspectives and gain insight into cultures beyond their own. Furthermore, platforms such as LinkedIn and Twitter have become invaluable professional tools, allowing users to utilize networking opportunities that were previously inaccessible. However, these advantages are not without cost. The prevalence of curated, idealized content has been shown to contribute to social comparison and a profound sense of detachment, particularly among younger users. Secondly, algorithmic echo chambers may reinforce existing biases, resulting in a polarized discourse. Nevertheless, when approached with critical awareness, social media undeniably has the potential to enrich both personal and professional life.

sophisticated vocabulary ✓ complex grammar ✓ nuanced argument ✓ idiomatic expressions ✓
