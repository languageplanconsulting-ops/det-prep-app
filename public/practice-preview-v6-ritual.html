// design decisions traced to evidence

  • Greeting bar identical every visit — mere-exposure effect → safety; reduces decision fatigue (Baumeister)
  • ONE pre-decided task, no choices — Hick's Law + default effect + Fogg BM (motivation is low, so ability bar must be near-zero)
  • Reason ("เมื่อวานน้องตอบถูก 4/10") shown before action — algorithmic transparency builds trust (Logg, Bonaccio); also models metacognition
  • Cream + soft borders, not brutalist black — color/cortisol research (Mehta & Zhu); trauma-informed safety signals
  • "ไม่ต้องรีบ ไม่มีผิดถูก" — anxiety-state reframe (Yerkes-Dodson); pull arousal back to optimal
  • Visible escape ("วันนี้พอแค่นี้") — control = safety for shame-state users (trauma-informed UX)
  • Goal-gradient shrinking bar with "+12 in 9 days" — Hull goal-gradient + endowed progress (no zero anywhere)
  • "Recent wins" before any dashboard — peak-end memory (Kahneman); mastery experiences are #1 self-efficacy source (Bandura)
  • Locks reframed "เปิดเมื่อ Basic" not "Locked" — reactance reduction (Brehm); gain framing not loss
  • Free-tier alert removed from main flow — restriction language depletes self-efficacy; reframed as "what you have" inside drawer instead
  • "คุณทำได้แน่นอน · ครูอยู่ด้วยตลอด" on CTA — verbal persuasion (Bandura #3); presence framing reduces solo-anxiety
  • 3 soft-link opt-ins, not 13 skill tiles — Cognitive Load Theory; extraneous load → near zero above fold
  • Bottom-of-card thumb-zone CTA — Hoober one-handed reach research
  • No English headers above the action — L2 readability; remove parse-stalls
  • Numbers in mono, Thai labels in Sarabun — readability research: monospace hurts Thai segmentation
