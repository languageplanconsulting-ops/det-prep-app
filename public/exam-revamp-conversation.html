📌 สถานการณ์ (5 ประโยค)

  1. Two coworkers are planning a team lunch.
  2. They disagree about the restaurant choice.
  3. One prefers Thai food; the other wants pizza.
  4. They consider everyone's dietary needs.
  5. Finally they decide to ask the whole team.

🗣️ บทสนทนา

A
AnnaHow about Thai food for the team lunch?
B
BenHmm, not everyone likes spicy food though.
A
AnnaGood point. Maybe we should ask the team first.
